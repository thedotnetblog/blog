Emiliano is a Microsoft MVP in Developer Technologies with a deep focus on .NET, Azure, AI, and cloud-native development. He's known for building creative real-world projects — from AI-powered traffic analyzers to intelligent Christmas spending trackers — and turning them into conference talks and blog posts that developers actually enjoy reading.

His recent work focuses on the intersection of AI and .NET, including Semantic Kernel, .NET Aspire, and Microsoft's Agent Framework. He's a Sessionize Most Active Speaker and has presented at events ranging from NDC to Global Azure and the .NET AI Community Standup.

When he's not building cloud solutions at Intelequia or speaking at conferences, you'll find him mentoring other developers and contributing to the open-source ecosystem.
